# NFL Fantasy Playoff Roster Plan (2024-2025 Season)

## League Rules Summary
- **Total Roster**: 9 players per week
- **Positions**: 1 QB, 2-3 RB, 2-3 WR, 1-2 TE, 0-1 PK, 0-1 DEF
- **Scoring**: PPR League (1.5 PPR for TE, 1 PPR for others)
- **Key Constraint**: Each player can only be used ONCE across all playoff weeks
- **Strategy**: Need to predict winners to manage player pool as losing teams are eliminated
